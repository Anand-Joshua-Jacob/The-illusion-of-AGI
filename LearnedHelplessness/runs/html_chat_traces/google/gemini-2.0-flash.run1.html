    • Ensure the tabletop is flat. You can use cauls (pieces of wood clamped across the boards) to help keep the boards aligned.

    • Tabletop fasteners: These metal fasteners are designed to allow for wood movement. Install them in the aprons and then screw them to the underside of the tabletop.
    • Wood blocks: Glue and screw small wood blocks to the inside of the aprons. Then screw the tabletop to these blocks.
