Step 2: Plan and Measure Determine the dimensions of your table (length, width, and height). A standard dining table is usually about 30 inches high. Sketch out your design and mark your cuts on the wood using your measuring tape and pencil.

Step 3: Cut the Wood Using your saw, carefully cut all your pieces to the measured lengths. You will need: * Boards for the tabletop * 4 legs * 4 apron pieces (2 long, 2 short) to connect the legs and support the top

Step 4: Assemble the Tabletop Lay the tabletop boards flat side-by-side. To join them securely, apply wood glue along the seams. For the strongest hold, use a pocket hole jig to drill holes along the edges of the boards and screw them together. Clamp the boards tightly together and wipe away any excess glue. Step 5: Build the Table Base Take the 4 legs and the 4 apron pieces. You are going to build a rectangular frame with the legs at the corners. Place the assembled base upside down on top of it, centering it so there is an even overhang on all sides.
